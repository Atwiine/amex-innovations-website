Who We Are

Empowering Communities Through Technology

Amex Innovations is a Ugandan technology company committed to building simple, effective, and impactful digital tools. Our mission is to help communities, businesses, and institutions work smarter, grow faster, and achieve more.

From empowering farmers through FarmEase, to improving healthcare efficiency with the ICT Inventory System (IIS), and helping labs run better with LabTrack — we design solutions that solve real challenges in agriculture, healthcare, and beyond. Every tool we build is practical, affordable, and designed with local needs in mind.

Local Context, Global Standards

We understand how communities in Uganda and East Africa actually work — and we build technology that fits that reality.

Simple Tools, Lasting Impact

We don't build complex systems for the sake of it. Every product we ship is easy to use, easy to maintain, and built to create measurable change.
