Who We Are

Empowering Innovation Through Technology

Amex Innovations is a Ugandan technology company committed to building simple, effective, and impactful digital tools.

Our mission is to help communities, businesses, and institutions work smarter, grow faster, and achieve more. We started in Mbarara with a straightforward belief — that good technology should not be complicated, expensive, or out of reach for the organizations that need it most.

From empowering farmers through FarmEase, to improving healthcare efficiency with the ICT Inventory System (IIS), and helping labs run better with LabTrack — we design solutions that solve real challenges in agriculture, healthcare, and beyond. Every tool we build is practical, affordable, and designed with local needs in mind.

Based in Mbarara, Uganda — working across East Africa
Deep experience in agriculture, healthcare, and education tech
Products deployed with Ministry of Health, NGOs, and farmers
Building for African problems — not adapting foreign templates

Mission, Vision & Values

These aren't statements we wrote for a pitch deck. They describe how we actually make decisions about what to build and who to serve.

Our Mission

To help communities, businesses, and institutions across Africa work smarter and grow faster — through simple, effective, and impactful digital tools designed around local realities, not imported assumptions.

Our Vision

A future where technology is not a privilege for well-funded organizations, but a practical tool available to every farmer, hospital, school, and community group that needs it to do their work well.

Our Values

Simplicity — build only what solves the problem. Honesty — tell clients what they need, not what sounds impressive. Impact — if it doesn't create measurable change, we're not building it.

Our Products

Three Products. Real Deployments.

We don't just consult and disappear. These are tools we have built, launched, and continue to maintain for real clients across Uganda.

ICT Inventory System (IIS)

Deployed with the Ministry of Health across hospitals in Uganda. IIS tracks every ICT asset — computers, printers, routers — across departments. Administrators always know what they have, where it is, and when it needs maintenance.

Know · Act · Improve

FarmEase

A digital platform built for smallholder farmers. FarmEase gives farmers a simple way to keep crop records, access market information, track expenses, and receive personalized growing advice — without needing expensive equipment or a strong internet connection.

Learn · Grow · Sell

LabTrack

A SaaS platform for laboratories to track equipment, log maintenance schedules, and monitor usage patterns. Labs using LabTrack stop losing equipment to poor record-keeping and catch maintenance issues before they become expensive breakdowns.

Organize · Monitor · Simplify
