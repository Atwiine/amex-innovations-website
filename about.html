Our Story

Started With One Question: Why Are Businesses Still Working This Hard?

Amex Innovations was founded in Kampala after watching too many businesses struggle — not because of lack of effort, but because they were working with the wrong tools.

Spreadsheets that broke. Paper records nobody could find. WhatsApp groups being used as inventory systems. Cash registers that gave you a receipt but couldn't tell you your actual profit. Manual reports that took days to produce and were already outdated by the time they were ready.

We started asking a different question. Instead of "what app can we sell you?", we asked: "How does your business actually work, and what would genuinely make it work better?" That question is still the foundation of everything we build today.

Every system is built from scratch — no recycled templates
Built and supported locally, right here in Kampala
Designed around how your team actually works
Staff training and ongoing support included in every project
Fair, transparent pricing — no hidden costs after sign-off

What Drives Us

Mission, Vision & Values

Three things we come back to every time we start a new project — no matter who the client is or how big the system.

Our Mission

To help businesses, institutions, and organizations across Uganda replace slow, manual, disorganized ways of working with digital systems that are practical, affordable, and genuinely useful — not just impressive in a demo.

Our Vision

A Uganda where every business — whether a corner shop, a hospital, a school, or a growing startup — has access to the kind of technology that helps it run properly, compete confidently, and grow with full visibility of its own numbers.

How We Work

Honest conversations. Realistic timelines. Fair pricing. We tell you what technology can and cannot do for your situation, we build what we agree on, and we stay accountable long after the project is complete.

Our Process

From Your First Call to Go-Live and Beyond

We follow a clear, honest process on every project — so you always know what's happening, what's next, and what it costs.

01
We Listen First

No proposals or quotes until we fully understand your operations, your challenge, and what success actually looks like for you.

02
We Design the Solution

We map out exactly what the system will do, how each part works, and what it will cost — in plain language, not technical jargon.

03
We Build in Phases

We develop your system in stages, showing you working progress throughout so you can give feedback before anything is finalized.

04
We Train Your Team

We don't hand over a system and disappear. Every person who will use it gets trained until they are fully comfortable — not just briefly shown.

05
We Stay With You

After launch we monitor the system, fix issues fast, and keep improving things as your business grows and your needs change.
